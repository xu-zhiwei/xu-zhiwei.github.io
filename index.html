About me

I am now a Ph.D. candidate in School of Software at Tsinghua University, co-supervised by Prof. Hai Wan and Prof. Xibin Zhao.

Prior to this, I received my bachelor's degree from School of Computer Engineering and Science, Shanghai University, and received my master's degree from School of Software at Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Min Zhou. I have served as a Research Intern in (1) Big Data and Networking (DataNET) Group since April 2019, supervised by Prof. Yang Chen, as well as (2) Lab of Services Computing and Data Mining (SCDM Lab) since September 2019, supervised by Prof. Guobing Zou.

Currently, my research interests include: software security, software engineering, and data-driven analysis for related topics.

[UPDATE on July 22, 2024]: I still have 1 open position for Master student in Class 2025. The admitted candidate will collaborate with me on data-driven security log analysis. Highly motivated students are warmly welcomed and feel free to reach out to me via email.

About me

I am now a first year M.Eng. student from School of Software at Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Min Zhou. During my B.S. journey, I served as a Research Intern in (1) Mobile Systems and Networking (MSN) group since April 2019, supervised by Prof. Yang Chen, as well as (2) Lab of Services Computing and Data Mining (SCDM Lab) since September 2019, supervised by Prof. Guobing Zou.

Currently, my research interests include

  • Static program analysis: develop more competitive static program analysis tools and dig deep into new approaches.

  • Information retrieve: study the effectiveness and efficiency of model for recommender systems and web search.

  • Edge services computing: investigate the edge computing's infrastructure and the machine learning on the edge to boost user satisfaction.

  • Online social networks: uncover special user behavior patterns and improve the performance of online platforms.

  • Data mining and interpretable AI: analyze the data and the model to provide suggestions and conclusions for interpretable machine learning.

NOTE: I am now actively seeking for an opportunity to be a PhD candidate starting 2024 (preferably outside China mainland), focusing on theory of machine learning and its applcations on recommender systems, etc. If you have any related opportunities, I’d really appreciate it if you could reach me out via email.

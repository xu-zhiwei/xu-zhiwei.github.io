About me

I am currently a 4-th year undergraduate (with honors) in School of Computer Science at Shanghai University (SHU). After that, I will go to School of Software of Tsinghua Univeristy to acquire my master's degree. During my B.S. journey, I served as a Research Intern in (1) Mobile Systems and Networking (MSN) group since April 2019, supervised by Prof. Yang Chen, as well as (2) Lab of Services Computing and Data Mining (SCDM Lab) since September 2019, supervised by Prof. Guobing Zou.

About Me
I am now a third-year undergraduate student (GPA Ranking: 1/165) in the School of Computer Science at Shanghai University. I currently work as a research assistant in the Lab of Service Computing and Data Mining (SCDM Lab) since 2019, supervised by Prof. Guobing Zou. My research interests lie in developing applied machine learning algorithms for graph-structured complex data, and their applications such as information retrieval, social network analysis, natural language processing and recommender systems.

About me

I am now a 4-th year undergraduate (with honors) from School of Computer Science at Shanghai University (SHU). During my B.S. journey, I served as a Research Intern in (1) Mobile Systems and Networking (MSN) group since April 2019, supervised by Prof. Yang Chen, as well as (2) Lab of Services Computing and Data Mining (SCDM Lab) since September 2019, supervised by Prof. Guobing Zou.

Currently, my research interests include

  • Online social networks: uncover special user behavior patterns and improve the performance of online platforms.

  • Edge services computing: investigate the edge computing's infrastructure and the machine learning on the edge to boost user satisfaction.

  • Data mining and interpretable AI: analyze the data and the model to provide suggestions and conclusions for interpretable machine learning.
